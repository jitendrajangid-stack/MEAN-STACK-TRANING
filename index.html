⚠️ Training ground rules — read every day

These are mandatory for the whole training. Please follow them daily.

  • 8 working hours every day. Treat it like a full working day.
  • Basecamp check-in / check-out. Post on Basecamp when your day starts, whenever you step out mid-day, and when you leave for the day.
  • Daily progress message. Share what you did each day on Basecamp — every day, without skipping.
  • Keep this tracker updated. Update your status & daily log here every day. A day with no update is marked as leave.
  • Questions & blockers → Basecamp before you leave. Post them the same day; your team lead will connect the next day to discuss.
  • You are being evaluated. Your daily progress and the final project decide your assessment; next steps are decided once the training ends.
  • Timeline — 45 days max. 30 training days → 5-day final project → 5 buffer days (catch-up / leave). Plan your pace to finish the whole thing within 45 days.

👋 Welcome — how this tracker works

A 2-minute read so you can start on your own. Reopen this anytime with the ❔ Guide button.

  1. Your board, in tabs. 6-Week Plan = what to learn each day · Project Build = a project you build in parallel · Final Project = the 5-day capstone · Daily Log · Queries · Resources.
  2. Mark your progress. In the Plan / Project / Final tabs, click the Status chip to cycle Not started → In progress → Done. It saves automatically.
  3. Work in order. Each day stays 🔒 Locked until you finish the previous day — so just go top to bottom.
  4. Fill the Daily Log every day. Planned vs actual hours, what you learned/built, blockers, and a 1–5 confidence. This feeds your score.
  5. See your progress at a glance. The ring + bars at the top show your overall %, and per-section completion.
  6. Stuck? Ask in Queries. Open the ❓ Queries tab, pick the related day, and post your question. Your team lead replies (usually next day) and marks it resolved — no need to chase anyone.
  7. Timeline. 30 training days → 5-day final project → 5 buffer days, and everything must finish within 45 days.
  8. You stay signed in across refresh and the next day until you hit Sign out. Progress is saved to the cloud, not just this device.

Each time you sign in you’ll also see the short daily ground rules (8h/day, Basecamp check-ins, keep this updated). Please follow them.
